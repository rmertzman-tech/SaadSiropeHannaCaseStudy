ATCF Analysis: Coherence Under Extreme Pressure

ATCF Components

ATCF (Adaptive Temporal Coherence Function) measures integration across time through four components:

  1. Historical Continuity (HC): Coherent integrated sense of past
  2. Present Integration (PI): Internal coherence right now - values/behavior aligned
  3. Prospective Coherence (PC): Ability to imagine authentic future
  4. Meta-Constructor Capacity (MCC): Ability to examine and modify your PRF

Scale: 0-10 for each component. Combined ATCF = average of four.
